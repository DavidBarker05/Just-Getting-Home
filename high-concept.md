# Just Getting Home

## Genre
2D Platformer

## Core Loop
1. Player spawns in the level.
2. Player sees the hero fighting with some kind of enemy.
3. Hero and enemy fight and destroy surroundings, which creates obstacles and hazards.
4. Hero defeats enemy and goes off screen.
5. Player must now get to the end of the level.
6. Player avoids obstacles and hazards created by the hero's fight.

## Visual Style
Simple 2D pixel art.
Medieval fantasy with basic towns made of stone and wood, stone/wood bridges, lots of trees and forests.

## Intended Experience
The player should feel like they are not the hero. They are an ordinary peasant trying to get home.
As the hero crosses the player’s path and battles enemies, the world changes around the player—turning the route home into a dangerous obstacle course.

## Levels (theme overview)
- Market: daytime chaos; debris from fighting disrupts stalls and sidewalks.
- Bridge: the battle changes a critical crossing, forcing the player to improvise their route.
- Forest choke point: the hero’s fight triggers falls and shifting hazards through trees and brush.
- Homecoming aftermath: the hero’s final battle destroys parts of the home environment, turning “home” into a hazard zone.
